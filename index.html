About Biwosen Trading PLC

Biwosen Trading PLC is a dynamic and reputable company engaged in diverse business activities. Our core operations include export trade in live animals and animal products, cutting and finishing of marble and limestone, wholesale trade in agricultural products, and vehicle assembly services.

Location: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Our Core Business Activities

Biwosen Trading PLC operates across export, import, and industrial services, delivering high-quality products and reliable solutions to both domestic and international markets.

Export

Agriculture

We are engaged in the export of high-quality agricultural products, carefully sourced to meet international standards. Our supply chain ensures consistency, freshness, and reliability for global markets.

Live Animals

We specialize in the export of live animals including cattle, camels, sheep, and goats. Our operations follow strict quality, health, and ethical standards to ensure safe and efficient delivery to international buyers.

Minerals

We export valuable mineral resources including gold, copper, and processed marble. Our mineral products meet international quality standards and are supplied to a wide range of industrial and commercial clients.

Import

Consumer Goods

We import a wide range of consumer goods including food products such as pasta, edible oils, and chocolates. Our focus is on delivering high-quality, affordable goods that meet the needs of local markets.

Services

Industrial Services

We provide a range of industrial services including vehicle assembly, heavy machinery operations, and stone processing. Our expertise in marble and limestone cutting, shaping, and finishing ensures high-quality results tailored to client requirements.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality products and services that meet the needs of our clients while fostering sustainable growth and development in Ethiopia. We are committed to ethical business practices, innovation, and continuous improvement in all our operations.

Vision

To become a leading global trading company recognized for our excellence in quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We aim to contribute significantly to the economic development of Ethiopia and the well-being of our stakeholders.
